## Time Intervals
When using the Period Comparison view, you can choose between several Time Intervals to display:

- Latest Day
- Current Month
- Previous Month
- Latest 30 Days
- Latest 3 months
- Latest 6 Months
- Latest 12 Months
- Year to Date
