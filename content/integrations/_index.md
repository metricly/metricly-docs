An integration is a system from which Metricly collects data. Metricly offers integrations with *collectors*, *agents*, and *automation software*.

## Common Dependencies

### OS
- Redhat 6 and 7
- CentOS 6 and 7
- Amazon Linux (latest)
- Ubuntu 12, 14, 15, and 16
- Debian 7, 8, and 9
